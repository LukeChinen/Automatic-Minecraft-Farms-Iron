Automatic Iron Farms


In Minecraft, iron is one of the most important materials, as it is used in almost all redstone builds, mining for rare materials, and is used in a various amount of blocks.
But through their importance, they are quite difficult to find and gather, as after stripping a cave of all of its iron, you would have to find another and anouther until you have a considerable amount of iron.
But thanks to automatic farms, gathering iron can now be an instantaneous process, where you wouldn't have to mine or smelt the iron ores.
Making these farms are quite tricky as you would have to be working with the greatly annoying villagers and zombies.
